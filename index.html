About Us.

Uni-fyed is an online language exchange platform for university students. Our mission is to enable students to find language partners that are on their own campus so that they can practice speaking the languages they are learning.

How it works.

Follow these four simple steps to find a language partner

1 Sign Up

Join the local Uni-fyed community by choosing your university from the drop-down menu. You will need to use your university email to sign-up.

2 Create a Profile

Your profile will enable users to see your language level, availability, interests & hobbies and more. This ensures that you can find the perfect language partner for you.

3 Search for Partners

Once your profile is completed, you can search for language partners in your community by clicking on the search bar. You can then view their profiles and send language partner requests.

4 Organise Exchange

Once your language partner request is accepted, you can message users through the platform to organise a language exchange.

Features

Language Profiles

Your language profile ensures that you can find a language partner with the same language level, interests & hobbies, and availability as you.

Search Filters

Our search filters enable you to find native speakers of the languages of the languages you are learning as well as students that are studying the same language as you.

Messaging

Send messages to your language partners through the platform to organise a language exchange.
